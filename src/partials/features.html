UPGRADE YOUR DEFENSE SYSTEM

Credits earned during missions can be used to improve the defense grid.

Players operate a military-style command interface responsible for intercepting incoming threats before they reach protected cities. The system must constantly track hostile projectiles while launching interceptors at precisely calculated moments.

It's all about timing and not wasting your shots. You can't just spam interceptors because you've got limited ammo, your systems need time to reload, and your radar can only track so much at once.
Success depends on maintaining control under pressure while coordinating the defense grid.
